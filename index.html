Opening Monero to the world

Haveno-reto is the flagship instance of Haveno, the non-custodial, decentralized exchange platform for crypto and fiat currencies built on Tor and Monero.

It doesn't get more private than that.



What Haveno-reto is:

  • The first fully functional Haveno instance on mainnet

  • A low arbitration fee network (0% taker, 0.6% maker)

  • A network with zero general fees
    (fees go exclusively to arbitrators)

  • Essentially Bisq1 adapted for Monero.


What Haveno-reto isn't:

  • Custodial
  • Centralized
  • The developers of the Haveno software
  • Officially endorsed by the Haveno developers
    (they don't endorse any instance of Haveno)
